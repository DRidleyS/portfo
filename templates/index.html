Intro

My name is Dorian Ridley-Smith and I am a 20 year old programmer trying to carve out a life for myself in California, USA

I live in Lake county California, a small town in northern California home to more than 30 famous wineries and endless breathtaking natural beauty interlaced with lively tight roads that make for endless enjoyment behind the wheel.

For many years I cursed at wires, problem solving was always the name of the game as a technician. I grew tenacious, I found joy in the struggle.


^thats a blown up disconnect. Below you can see also a partially vaporized breaker.

Ill always love the thrill of building things in the physical world, and I'm never gonna stop!

But after dabbling in Andrei Neagoie's "Python - Zero To Mastery" course for just a day, I was hooked. The same day I told my little brother Zach that I was going to learn this new skill, and from that point on we buried our heads in the coursework to no end. I am endlessly thankful to Andrei and his team for making such an incredible curriculum, and I commend Andrei for being such a skilled teacher.

Work

I currently hold a Certificate of completion of the "Complete Python Developer in 2024: Zero to Mastery" course offered by Andrei Neagoie through his accredited school ZTM and Udemy.


This was a very well constructed course which proved to be both engaging and instructive. It comprehensively covered essential tools, techniques, and best practices crucial for establishing a foundation as a professional developer. The curriculum spanned from fundamental concepts delineating the structure of the Python language to the cultivation of skills and habits essential for effective problem-solving. Consequently, I acquired the proficiency required to adeptly identify and employ needed libraries and modules for diverse projects.


one of my favourite projects in the Complete Python Developer course is this password checker similar to haveibeenpwned.com



This was a very small project but I thought it was fantastic at demonstrating the importance of security, especially when dealing with sensitive information like passwords.

I am also currently enrolled in "Complete Web Developer in 2024: Zero to Mastery" and Andrei's web security course, amongst a few more that will be included here upon completion.


About

Dorian's Journey:



Shifting from Electrics to Code in California.

Hey there, I'm Dorian—a California guy with English roots. Grew up messing around with electronics, thanks to my dad. Started off as an electrician, got the hang of it pretty well.

Lately, though, I've switched gears. Now, I'm diving into Python programming and web development. Coding's the new gig, and I'm really enjoying the challenge it brings.

Outside of the tech world, I'm all about street cars. Love building them and driving them at the limit. There's just something about the visceral feeling of a machine built for speed that is euphoric to me.

As for the future, I'm working on becoming a solid developer, aiming for the flexibility of remote work. That way, I can spend more time in my garage, fine-tuning my street cars. It's a process, but I'm making my way.

Life's a mix of coding puzzles and the grease on my hands from the garage. I'm rolling with it, guided by the idea of a job I enjoy and a garage full of awesome cars.
